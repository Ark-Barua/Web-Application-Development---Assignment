Pension Samvaad

What is Pension Samvaad?

Pension Samvaad is a comprehensive communication and grievance redressal platform designed to facilitate direct dialogue between pensioners, government employees, and the pension administration. It serves as a bridge for effective communication and quick resolution of pension-related issues.

Key Features

Direct Communication

Direct interaction with pension authorities for quick resolution

Grievance Redressal

Dedicated platform for lodging and tracking complaints

Policy Updates

Regular updates on pension policies and procedures

Community Support

Connect with fellow pensioners and share experiences

Services Available

Grievance Lodging

  • Online complaint submission
  • Document upload facility
  • Real-time tracking of complaints
  • Status updates via SMS/Email

Policy Information

  • Latest pension policy updates
  • Circulars and notifications
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Guidelines and procedures

Interactive Sessions

  • Monthly webinars on pension topics
  • Expert consultation sessions
  • Q&A sessions with authorities
  • Training programs for staff
