About Pension Functions

Overview

The Pension Division of the Principal Accountant General (A&E) - I, Maharashtra, Mumbai is responsible for the administration and management of pension benefits for government employees. Our functions encompass the entire lifecycle of pension processing from application to disbursement.

Core Functions

Pension Application Processing

Receiving, scrutinizing, and processing pension applications from government employees

Pension Calculation

Computing pension benefits based on service records and government rules

Family Pension Management

Processing family pension applications and managing beneficiary entitlements

Pension Disbursement

Ensuring timely and accurate payment of pension benefits

Administrative Functions

Document Verification

  • Verification of service records and certificates
  • Validation of retirement documents
  • Scrutiny of family pension claims
  • Authentication of beneficiary documents

Record Management

  • Maintenance of pension records
  • Digital archiving of documents
  • Regular updates of pensioner information
  • Historical data preservation

Compliance Monitoring

  • Ensuring adherence to pension rules
  • Monitoring regulatory compliance
  • Audit trail maintenance
  • Policy implementation oversight

Service Delivery Functions

Customer Support

Providing assistance to pensioners and applicants through various channels including helpline, email, and in-person support.

Grievance Redressal

Addressing pension-related complaints and ensuring timely resolution of issues raised by pensioners.

Policy Implementation

Implementing government pension policies and ensuring their proper execution across all departments.

Training and Awareness

Conducting training programs for staff and awareness sessions for pensioners about pension rules and procedures.

Quality Assurance

Our pension functions are governed by strict quality standards to ensure:

  • Accuracy in pension calculations
  • Timeliness in processing applications
  • Transparency in all operations
  • Compliance with government regulations
  • Efficient grievance resolution
  • Secure handling of sensitive information

Performance Metrics

Processing Time

Target: 90% of applications processed within 30 days

Accuracy Rate

Target: 99.5% accuracy in pension calculations

Customer Satisfaction

Target: 95% satisfaction rate among pensioners

Grievance Resolution

Target: 100% resolution within 15 working days
