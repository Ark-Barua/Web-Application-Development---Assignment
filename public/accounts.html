Accounts Division

Overview

The Accounts Division is responsible for maintaining and auditing the accounts of various government departments and entities in Maharashtra. We ensure financial transparency, accountability, and compliance with government accounting standards.

Core Functions

Financial Accounting

Maintenance of accounts for government departments, public sector undertakings, and autonomous bodies.

Audit Services

Comprehensive audit of financial statements and compliance with government regulations.

Financial Reporting

Preparation and submission of financial reports to government authorities.

Compliance Monitoring

Ensuring adherence to financial rules, regulations, and government policies.

Services Offered

Departmental Accounts

  • Maintenance of accounts for various government departments
  • Preparation of monthly and annual financial statements
  • Reconciliation of accounts
  • Budget monitoring and control

Audit Services

  • Internal audit of government departments
  • Compliance audit
  • Performance audit
  • Special audit assignments

Financial Advisory

  • Financial planning and budgeting
  • Cost analysis and optimization
  • Financial risk assessment
  • Implementation of financial controls
