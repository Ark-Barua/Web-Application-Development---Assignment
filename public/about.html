About Us

Our Mission

The Principal Accountant General (A&E) - I, Maharashtra, Mumbai is committed to ensuring transparency, accountability, and efficiency in government financial operations. We serve as the premier accounting and auditing authority for the state of Maharashtra.

Our Vision

To be a world-class accounting and auditing organization that promotes good governance and financial integrity in public administration.

Core Functions

Audit & Accounts

Comprehensive audit of government departments and maintenance of accounts for various government entities.

Pension Management

Processing and disbursement of pension benefits to government employees and their families.

GPF Administration

Management of General Provident Fund accounts and related financial services.

Financial Oversight

Ensuring compliance with financial regulations and maintaining fiscal discipline.
